Project Concept
Communio: Welcome To Symbiosis： is an interactive installation that transforms audience members’ facial and voice data into digital cells.

Each face generates the cell’s visual form, while each voice determines its behaviour. These cells are then placed into a virtual petri dish, where they grow, float, merge, and evolve.

The word “communio” means shared participation and contribution. In this project, each audience member offers part of themselves to a collective digital organism.

The work reflects how people today continuously upload personal data into AI and algorithmic systems, where individual traces are processed, combined, and transformed into something unpredictable.
